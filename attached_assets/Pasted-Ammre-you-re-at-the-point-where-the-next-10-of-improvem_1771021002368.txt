The next step is to merge these into a single borrower graph with nodes like:

2. Real-Time Eligibility Engine (Mini-AUS)

You already have tiered trust data.
Now add a rules engine that evaluates:

• DTI
• LTV
• Reserves
• Employment stability
• Credit tiers
• AUS-like conditions


This becomes a real-time eligibility engine that updates as the user chats, uploads documents, or browses properties.

Why it matters

• You can show “Eligible for X loan types” dynamically
• You can pre-qualify users before they apply
• You can match properties to what they can afford
• You can reduce LO workload by 40–60%
