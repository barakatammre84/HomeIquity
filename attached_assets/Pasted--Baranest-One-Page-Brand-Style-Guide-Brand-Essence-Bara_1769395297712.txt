# Baranest – One-Page Brand Style Guide

## Brand Essence

**Baranest** is a modern mortgage platform designed to make homeownership feel clear, calm, and achievable.

**Brand Promise:** A simpler way to get home‑ready.

**Personality:**

* Calm
* Trustworthy
* Clear
* Human
* Confident (never flashy)

Think: *Better.com clarity × Zillow familiarity × modern banking trust*

---

## Logo Usage

**Primary Logo:** Wordmark only – `baranest`

* Lowercase preferred
* No icons required at MVP stage
* Plenty of white space around the logo

**Do not:**

* Add roof/house/key icons
* Stretch or outline the logo
* Place on busy backgrounds

---

## Typography

**Primary Font:** Inter (preferred) or DM Sans

**Headings:**

* H1: 32–40px
* H2: 24–28px
* H3: 18–20px

**Body Text:**

* 14–16px
* Line height: 1.5–1.6

**Tone:** Plain English. No jargon. No hype.

---

## Buttons & CTAs

### Primary CTA

* Background: Nest Blue
* Text: White
* Radius: 10–12px

**Examples:**

* Get Pre‑Approved
* Continue
* Upload Documents

### Secondary CTA

* Border: Nest Blue
* Text: Nest Blue
* Background: Transparent

### Disabled State

```
Background: #CBD5E1
Text: #94A3B8
```

---

## UI Style Guidelines

* Rounded corners everywhere
* Soft shadows, no harsh borders
* Clean cards with breathing room
* Guided, step‑by‑step flows

**Card Style Example:**

```
border-radius: 12px;
box-shadow: 0 8px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.06);
background: #FFFFFF;
```

---

## Voice & Microcopy

**Voice:** Reassuring, direct, supportive

**Good Examples:**

* You’re on track
* Just one more step
* We’ll let you know if anything changes

**Avoid:**

* Guaranteed
* Instant approval
* AI‑powered claims
* Over‑technical language

---

## Borrower vs Lender UI Emphasis

**Borrower Experience:**

* Warmer (more Sage)
* Plain language
* Progress reassurance

**Lender / Ops Experience:**

* More Nest Blue
* Data‑forward tables
* Confidence scores visible

Same brand. Different focus.

---

## Final Guiding Principle

> If it feels confusing, crowded, or stressful — it’s not Baranest.

Clarity always wins.
