The Correct AI Control Loop
1. Intake (Structured, Explainable)
The AI gathers:

Income sources

Employment details

Asset types

Property intent

Household composition

Always framed as:

“This information is required by underwriting systems to evaluate eligibility.”

2. Verification Readiness (Not Approval)
The AI checks:

Is the data complete?

Is it internally consistent?

Are documents present and current?

Are formats acceptable?

It never says “approved.”
It says:

“This item is required before underwriting can proceed.”

3. Gap Identification (This Is Where You Win)
Instead of deciding, the AI flags gaps:

Missing documents

Expired statements

Inconsistent income

Unverified assets

This is purely procedural, not judgmental.

4. Packaging for Underwriting Systems
The AI outputs:

Structured borrower summary

Document index

Readiness status

Verification notes

This feeds:

LOS systems

Underwriting engines

Human underwriters
