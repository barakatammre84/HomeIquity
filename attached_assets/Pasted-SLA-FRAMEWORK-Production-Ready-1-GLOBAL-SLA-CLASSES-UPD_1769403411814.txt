1️⃣ GLOBAL SLA CLASSES (UPDATED)
SLA Class	Target Resolution	Escalation Starts	Hard Breach
S0 – Immediate	0–1 hour	15 minutes	1 hour
S1 – Critical	4 hours	2 hours	4 hours
S2 – High	12 hours	8 hours	12 hours
S3 – Standard ✅	48 hours	36 hours	48 hours
S4 – Low ✅	72 hours	60 hours	72 hours
S5 – Informational	N/A	None	None

📌 Only S0–S3 block loan progression
📌 S4 tasks never block — they surface risk or completeness only
